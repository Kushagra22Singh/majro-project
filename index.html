Why this concept matters

Many crop decisions happen quickly and directly in the field. Leaflens is designed to keep important information visible, readable, and actionable on a phone-sized screen.

What the current demo includes

  • Separate HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files for easier project scaling.
  • Clearer hierarchy between hero content, feature cards, and interactive tools.
  • Responsive panels that stay usable on desktop and mobile layouts.
